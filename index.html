Our Story

Founded in 2015, Luméra Jewels began as a small atelier in Milan, Italy — where craftsmanship met creativity. Over the years, we've expanded globally, maintaining our promise of delivering luxury that lasts forever.

Each piece is designed with detail, handcrafted by artisans who believe jewelry is more than beauty — it’s legacy.

The Visionary Behind Luméra

Isabella Renaldi, the founder, turned her passion for gemstones into a world-renowned brand. Her philosophy: luxury isn't just what you wear, it's how it makes you feel.

Every collection reflects her dedication to perfection and the belief that every piece tells a story.
